About US

Our Story

In March 2022 we opened our doors in Revature World, with a clear focus on customer service as we offered the very first complete vacation package. Soon after, we were helping people discover the world with international trips. Today, from being a part of one of the world’s largest travel companies, Flight Centre Travel Group to supporting small travel businesses with Independent by Excelsior Travel Agency, our commitment to creating connections, providing one-on-one service, and crafting the perfect vacation remains stronger than ever.
